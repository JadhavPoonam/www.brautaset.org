About

I grew up in Hovdebygda, a village in Ørsta Municipality on the west coast of Norway. I studied Electronics in Ørsta, Ålesund & Oslo. After interrupting my studies by compulsory military service I moved to London to study Artificial Intelligence at Westminster University in 2000. I still live in England, though in March 2015 I moved from London to Berwick-upon-Tweed.

I have worked in software development since I graduated in 2003. Over the years employers have paid me to write HTML, PHP, Perl, C, JavaScript, Java, Objective-C, Scala, Python, Bash and English.
